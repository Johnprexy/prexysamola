About Prexy Sam Ola

Discovering the heart behind the music

A Heart for Worship

Prexy Sam Ola is a passionate gospel artist based in the USA, originally from Nigeria, who has dedicated his life to spreading the love of God through powerful worship music. With a heart deeply rooted in faith and a voice anointed by the Holy Spirit, he continues to touch lives and transform hearts across the globe.

Born and raised in Nigeria, Prexy Sam Ola discovered his calling to ministry at an early age. His musical journey began in his local church, where he served as a worship leader and developed his unique style of blending traditional gospel with contemporary worship elements.

After relocating to the United States, he has been blessed to minister in various churches, conferences, and gospel events, always with the mission of drawing people closer to God through authentic worship experiences.

Ministry & Mission

Prexy Sam Ola's ministry is built on the foundation of authentic worship and genuine relationship with God. He believes that music is not just entertainment, but a powerful tool for spiritual transformation and healing. His songs are born from personal experiences with God and are designed to lead listeners into His presence.

Through his music, Prexy Sam Ola seeks to:

  • Create an atmosphere of worship that draws people into God's presence
  • Encourage believers in their faith journey
  • Reach the lost with the message of God's love and salvation
  • Bridge cultural gaps through the universal language of worship

His passion for worship extends beyond just music performance; he is committed to raising up a generation of worshippers who understand the true essence of worship and their identity in Christ.
